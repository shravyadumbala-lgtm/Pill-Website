Simple by design

Set it up in 3 minutes

📦

Fill your bottle

Transfer your pills into PillPal. The built-in precision scale automatically weighs the contents and calculates how many pills you have — no manual counting needed.

📱

Set your schedule

Open the PillPal app and enter your prescription details. We'll build a personalized dose schedule and enable push reminders that sync directly with the bottle's display.

🔒

Stay protected

If the cap opens outside a dose window, PillPal sounds an audible alarm and notifies your phone. Perfect for households with children or anyone managing complex regimens.
